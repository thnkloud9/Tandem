Rating Recordings

Rating recordings helps other Tandem users by giving them feedback on their speaking skills, but also improves your listening skills by giving you an opportunity to hear a mix of both native speakers and fellow languge learners. The rating system is a standard 5 star system but each star has a specific meaning. Please keep in mind that sometimes every day speech doesn't always follow perfect grammer rules, and sometimes slang will be used, and infact preferred by some language learners.

Commenting Recording

Use comments to give specific feedback to users about their pronunciation, grammer, and word choice. You can also use comments to ask questions and learn more yourself, if you hear something you don't understand, like a slang phrase or idiom.

Transcribing Recording

To improve your listening skills you can transcribe answer audio. Just listen to the recording and type in what you hear. If you can not understand the audio grade the answer accordingly, or use comments to request clarification if you are unsure wether or not something is correct, or its just a phrase or word you are not familiar with.

Adding a Question to a Practice Set

If you'd like to include a question in one of your Practice Sets you'll need to record your own audio for the question in both your spoken and learning language**. If you are not comfortable with the pronunciation of the question in your learning language you can play existing audio for the question.

Once you have recorded audio for both your spoken and learning languages you will then be able to add the question to one of your existing Practice Sets.
